Game Controls & Mechanics

  • WASD Use these keys to move your car in all directions
  • Crystals Collect glowing crystals to earn points (+10 points each)

  • Power-ups Collect purple orbs for temporary speed boosts

Tip: The game gets progressively harder as your score increases. Plan your movements carefully!
